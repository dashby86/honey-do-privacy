Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Honey Do! This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our collaborative todo list application. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal data.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account
  • Anonymous Usage: If you use the app as a guest, we create an anonymous user ID to manage your data

App Data

  • Todo Lists and Items: The tasks, lists, and notes you create in the app
  • List Sharing: Information about shared lists and collaboration with other users
  • Assignment Data: Task assignments and member information for shared lists

Technical Information

  • Device Tokens: Firebase Cloud Messaging tokens for push notifications
  • Usage Analytics: App performance and crash reports through Firebase Analytics
  • Authentication Data: Login sessions and security tokens

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide Core Features: Store and sync your todo lists across devices
  • Enable Collaboration: Share lists with other users and manage permissions
  • Send Notifications: Alert you about list updates and assignments
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and performance
  • Security: Protect your account and prevent unauthorized access
  • Support: Help resolve technical issues and respond to inquiries

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Authentication: Secure user authentication prevents unauthorized access
  • Access Control: Users can only access lists they've created or been invited to
  • Secure Infrastructure: Firebase provides enterprise-grade security and compliance
  • Data Location: Data is stored on Google's secure servers

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We only share data in these limited circumstances:

  • Firebase/Google: We use Google Firebase for data storage, authentication, and analytics
  • List Sharing: When you share a list, other members can see the list content and your name
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights and users
  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help us operate the app under strict confidentiality

Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app
  • Update: Edit your profile information and lists at any time
  • Delete: Remove lists, tasks, or your entire account
  • Export: Request a copy of your data
  • Notifications: Control push notification preferences in your device settings
  • Account Deletion: Request account deletion here or contact us directly

Children's Privacy

Honey Do is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

International Users

If you are using Honey Do from outside the United States, please note that your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States where Firebase servers are located. By using the app, you consent to this transfer.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or by email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.
